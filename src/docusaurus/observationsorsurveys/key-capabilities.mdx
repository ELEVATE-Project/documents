# Key Capabilities of Observations/Surveys

Using the <ProductName /> building block, organizations and individuals can effectively collect genuine data from numerous stakeholders. Moreover, these capabilities serve as valuable tools for users to gain insights and get a health card of different entities.

<table>
<tr>
    <th>Features</th>
    <th>Description</th>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>Question Types</td>
    <td>Various question types can be formulated, such as single-select questions, multi-select questions, date entry queries, text-only prompts, numerical queries, and slider-type questions.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>Evidence in Questions</td>
    <td>Users can attach evidence in the form of images, videos, and documents to support their responses.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>Hints and Tips</td>
    <td>Hints and tips alongside the questions improve user engagement and data accuracy. These guidance cues are designed to assist participants in effectively recording their responses, ensuring a smoother experience.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>Question Map</td>
    <td>The question map provides a bird's-eye view of the number of questions that are part of the observation. Clicking on any question number will take the respondent to that specific question.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>Remarks</td>
    <td>Users can add additional information as part of their responses in the form of remarks for any question.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>Save Progress</td>
    <td>Users can save their progress at any juncture, offering flexibility and convenience throughout the survey process. This feature empowers participants to pause and resume at their convenience, ensuring a user-friendly experience.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>Submit Responses</td>
    <td>Users can submit their surveys or observations after adding their responses to questions.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>Survey Reports</td>
    <td>After submitting the survey, users can access reports containing their responses to questions.</td>
</tr>
</table>
