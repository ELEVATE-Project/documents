## Introduction

Institutions or organisations across various sectors frequently confront substantial challenges in the collection of large-scale data. Whether in the education sector, healthcare sector, or other domains, leaders have the responsibility of gathering data to gain insights into areas that require improvement. This data-driven approach facilitates informed decision-making. 

The primary obstacle lies in acquiring authentic and comprehensive data that accurately represents the status of each entity within the given sector. Establishing context and collating evidentiary proof is nearly impossible. This gap needs to be addressed to gather authentic and genuine data that gives visibility at different levels. 

<ProductName /> capabilities address this challenge and provide a solution that revolutionizes data collection. The capability is NDEAR compliant and offer an innovative solution to the problem of collecting data from a large number of people. Using these advanced technological capabilities, various organizations, government agencies,  and institutions can now gather information and feedback on a much larger scale and at regular frequencies.

## Salient Features of Surveys

Surveys serve as a means to gather feedback and opinion from respondents. These surveys can be used to  evaluate the effectiveness of various aspects like services, training needs, and feedback. Typically, surveys are time-bound and accessible only once for respondents to complete. Using this capability, leaders and professionals across different sectors can easily participate in surveys customized to their specific roles and responsibilities.

With the ability to have a contextual set of questionnaires, surveys become relevant for all sectors in the data collection process. It will help gather data with speed and at scale.
