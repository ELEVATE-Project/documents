<li>On the dialog displaying the information about the meeting platform, do one of the following actions:</li>
    <ul>
    <li>If the meeting platform is BigBlueButton, click <b>Join</b>. The BigBlueButton window appears on your web browser.</li>
    <li>If the meeting platform is Google Meet, click <b>Join</b>. The Google Meet window appears.</li>
    <li>If the meeting platform is WhatsApp, click <b>Join</b>. The meeting appears on WhatsApp.</li>
    <li>If the meeting platform is Zoom, do one of the following actions:</li>
        <ul>
        <li>Click <b>Join</b>. The Zoom meeting window appears.</li>
        <li>Click Copy <img src={CopyIcon}/> to copy the <b>Meeting ID</b> and <b>Password</b> and paste it on the Zoom app to join the meeting.</li>
        </ul>
    </ul>
