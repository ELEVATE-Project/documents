# Logging in

Signed-up users can log in to access the Home page.

<b>To log in to the Home page, do as follows:</b>

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="mentored mobile application" label="Mobile App" default>
<ol>
<li>Open <PartialExample mentored /> and select <b>Login</b>.</li>
<li> Enter your <b>Email</b> and <b>Password</b>, and tap <b>Login</b>. The Home page appears.
    <Admonition type="note">
    <p>Enter the correct email ID and password to access the Home Page. Passwords are case-sensitive.</p>
    </Admonition>
</li>
</ol>
</TabItem> 
<TabItem value="mentored portal" label="Web Portal">
<ol>
<li>Open <PartialExample mentored /> and select <b>Login</b>.</li>
<li>Enter your <b>Email</b> and <b>Password</b>.
    <Admonition type="note">
    Enter the correct email ID and password to access the Home Page. Passwords are case-sensitive.
    </Admonition>
    <Admonition type="tip">  
    <p>Select the <b>Remember me</b> checkbox, to save your login credentials on the web browser.</p>
    </Admonition>
</li>
<li>Click <b>Login</b>. The Home page appears.</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Resetting the Password

While logging in, if you don't remember your password you can change it.

<b>To reset your password, do as follows:</b>

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="mentored mobile application" label="Mobile App" default>
<ol>
<li>On the Login page, tap <b>Forgot password</b>.</li>
<li>Enter your <b>Email</b> and the new password.</li>
<li>Tap <b>Reset Password</b>.</li>
<li>Enter the OTP sent to your registered email ID.
    <Admonition type="tip">   
    <p>To receive a new OTP, tap <b>Resend OTP</b>.</p>
    </Admonition>  
</li>
<li>Tap <b>Verify and login</b> to log in to the Home page.</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
<TabItem value="mentored portal" label="Web Portal">
<ol>
<li>On the Login page, click the <b>Forgot password</b> link.</li>
<li>Enter your <b>Email</b> and the new password.</li>
<li>Click <b>Reset Password</b>.</li>
<li>Enter the OTP sent to your registered email ID.
    <Admonition type="tip">    
    <p>To receive a new OTP, click the <b>Didn't receive? Send another code</b> link.</p>
    </Admonition>  
</li>
<li>Do the following actions:
    <ol>
    <li>To read the privacy policy, click the <b>Privacy Policy</b> link.</li>
    <li>To read the terms of service, click the <b>Terms of Service</b> link.</li>
    </ol>
</li>
<li>Select the checkbox to agree to the terms of service and policy settings.</li>
<li>Click <b>Verify and login</b> to log in to the Home page.</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
