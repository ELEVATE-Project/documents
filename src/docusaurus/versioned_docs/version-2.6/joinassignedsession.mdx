# Joining Sessions Assigned to Mentors

Session managers can join sessions assigned to mentors as a regular mentee. After joining the session, you can monitor the session and make the necessary changes to the session such as changing the meeting information.

## Enrolling for the Session

Before joining a Public or Private session, you must enroll for the session.

<p>  
    <Admonition type="tip">
    <p>Session managers can enroll for sessions that are assigned to a mentor even if the session seats are filled by enrolled and invited mentees.</p>
    </Admonition>
</p>

**To enroll for a session, do as follows:**

1. Do one of the following actions:

    * Select <b>Workspace</b> from the <b>Application</b> menu.

    * Go to the <b>Application</b> menu ![burger menu](media/burgermenu-icon.png) and select <b>Workspace</b>.

2. Click **Manage sessions**. The Session list section appears.

3. Find the session and click ![](media/sessionlist-view.png). The Session Details page appears.

4. Click **Enroll**. The session is listed on the <b>Enrolled sessions</b> tab of the Home page.

   <p>  
    <Admonition type="tip">
    <p>After enrolling, the <b>Mentee count</b> remains unchanged on the Session Details page, while your name is listed on the Mentee list window. See the following sections to learn more:</p>
    <ul>
    <li><a href="manageassignedsession#tracking-the-number-of-enrollments">Tracking the Number of Enrollments</a></li>
    <li><a href="manageassignedsession#viewing-the-list-of-mentees">Viewing the List of Mentees</a></li>
    </ul>
    </Admonition>
    </p>

    ![](media/assignedsessiondetails.png)

<p>  
<Admonition type="info">
<p>To explore other ways of enrolling for a Public session, see <a href="enrolling-for-a-session">Enrolling for a Session</a>.</p>
</Admonition>
</p>

### Cancelling the Enrollment

**To cancel your enrollment for a Public or Private session, do as follows:**

1. On the Session list section, find the enrolled session and click ![](media/sessionlist-view.png).

2. On the Session Details page, click **Unenroll**.

3. Click **Unenroll** on the confirmation dialog.

<p>  
<Admonition type="info">
<p>Session managers can also use the <b>Enrolled sessions</b> tab to cancel your enrollment for a Public session. See <a href="enrolling-for-a-session#cancelling-your-enrollment">Cancelling Your Enrollment</a> to learn more.</p>
</Admonition>
</p>
