# Sharing Session Invites to Mentees

You can invite participants to your session by sharing a session link.

**To share a session link, do as follows:**

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="mentored mobile application" label="Mobile App" default>
<ol>
<li>On the Home page, go to the <b>Created sessions</b> tab.</li>
<li>Find a session and tap the session tile.</li>
<li>Tap <b>Share</b> <div class="inlineImg"><img src={ShareIcon} /></div>. 
<br />
<br />
<img src={ShareSessionHost} />
</li>
<li>Share the session link with your contacts or on social media.</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
<TabItem value="mentored portal" label="Web Portal">
<ol>
<li>Go to the <b>Created sessions</b> tab.</li>
<li>Find a session and do one of the following actions: 
    <ul>
    <li>Click the session tile to view the Session Details page.</li>
    <li>Click the <b>View details</b> link to view the Session Details page.</li>
    </ul>
</li> 
<li>Click <b>Share Session</b>.</li>
<li>To copy the shareable link, click <b>Copy link</b>. 
<br />
<br />
<img src={PortalShareSessionHost} />
</li>
<li>Share the session link with your contacts or on social media.</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
