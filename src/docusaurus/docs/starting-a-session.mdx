# Starting a Session 

You can host the mentoring session through your selected meeting platform.

<Admonition type="note">
<ul>
<li>You can start the session only 10 minutes before the scheduled time.</li>
<li>You can start the session anytime within the time allocated for the session.</li>
<li>To start the session using the MentorED mobile application, you should install all the meeting applications except BigBlueButton.</li>
</ul>
</Admonition>

You can start a session in one of the following ways:

* Using the **Start** button
* Using the session tile
* Using the shared link

## Configuring the BigBlueButton Settings

During the mentoring session, you can configure the following BigBlueButton meeting settings:

* Configure the microphone.
* Configure other participants' microphones.
* Configure the webcam.
* Disable session recording.

### Configuring Your Microphone

1. To connect your microphone, click the **Microphone** option and select **Yes**. 

   <Admonition type="note"> 
   <p>Microphone may turn on by default.</p>
   </Admonition>

2.	To mute your microphone, click the **Microphone** button. Click again to unmute it.

### Configuring the Participants' Microphones

1. Go to the **User list toggle**.

2. To mute the microphone, do any one of the following actions:

   * Click the **Manage users** icon and select **Mute all users except presenter** to mute the participants' microphones.

   * Click the participant's name and select **Mute user** to mute a specific participant's microphone.

### Configuring the Webcam

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="mentored mobile application" label="Mobile App" default>
<ol>
<li>To turn on the webcam, tap the <b>Webcam</b> button.</li>
<li>Tap <b>Start sharing</b>. Before turning on your webcam, you can change the webcam (you can use either your front or rear camera of your phone) and background. 
<p>  
   <Admonition type="note">
   <ul>
   <li>The webcam may turn on by default.</li>
   <li>To change your webcam and background, tap the <b>Webcam</b> button, make changes, and close the window.</li>
   </ul>
   </Admonition>
</p>
</li>
<li>To turn it off, tap the <b>Webcam</b> button and tap <b>Stop sharing</b>.</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
<TabItem value="mentored portal" label="Web Portal" default>
<ol>
<li>To turn on the webcam, click the <b>Webcam</b> button.</li>
<li>Click <b>Start sharing</b>. Before turning on your webcam, you can change the webcam and background settings. 
<p> 
   <Admonition type="note">
   <ul>
   <li>The webcam may turn on by default.</li>
   <li>To change your webcam and background, do as follows:
      <ol>
      <li>Click the upward arrow on the <b>Webcam</b> button.</li>
      <li>Select <b>Open advanced settings</b> option.</li>
      <li>Change the background and close the Webcam settings window.</li>
      </ol>
   </li>
   </ul>
   </Admonition>
</p>
</li>
<li>To turn it off, click the <b>Webcam</b> button.</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
</Tabs>

### Checking the Quality of Your Internet Connection 

The **Connection status** icon located on the top portion of the window indicates the quality of your internet connection.

### Disabling Session Recording

To pause the recording, click the **Record** button and click **Yes**. Click the button again to resume recording.

### Ending the Session

**To end the mentoring session, do as follows:**

1. Go to the **Options** menu and select **End meeting**.

2. Click **End session for all users** button.

3. Click **Ok**. The <PartialExample mentored /> Home page appears and the session moves to the Past sessions section of the **Created Sessions** tab.

### Best Practices

- Unmute your [microphone](#configuring-the-microphone) only when necessary.

- Maintain a neat background and attire before you turn on your [webcam](#configuring-the-webcam). 

- Use respectful language while sharing any information. Discuss only mentoring-related topics.
