# Signing up as a Mentor

First-time users should create an account on <PartialExample mentored />.

<b>To sign up as a mentor, do as follows:</b>

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="mentored mobile application" label="Mobile App" default>
<ol>
<li>Install the <PartialExample mentored /> application from Google Play Store.</li>
<li>Open <PartialExample mentored /> and select <b>Sign up</b>.</li>
<li> Select <b>Mentor</b> and tap <b>Continue</b>.</li> 
<li>Enter your details:
    <ul>
    <li><b>Name</b></li>
    <li><b>Email</b>
    <p>
        <Admonition type="note">
        <p>Enter a valid email ID.</p>
        </Admonition>
    </p>
    </li>
    <li><b>Password</b></li>
    <li><b>Secret code</b></li>
    </ul>
</li>
<li>Tap on <b>Sign up as mentor</b>.</li>
<li>Enter the OTP sent to your registered email ID and tap <b>Verify and login</b>.</li>
<p>
    <Admonition type="tip">    
    <p>To receive a new OTP, tap <b>Resend OTP</b>.</p>
    </Admonition> 
</p> 
<li>On the Terms of Use page, review the terms of use and policy settings.</li>
<li>Select the checkbox to agree to the terms of use and policy settings.</li>
<li>Tap <b>Proceed</b>. The Home page appears.
<p>
    <img src={MentorHome} />
</p>
</li>
</ol>   
</TabItem>
<TabItem value="mentored portal" label="Web Portal">
<ol>
<li>Open <PartialExample mentored /> and select <b>Sign Up</b>.</li>
<li> Select <b>I'm a Mentor</b> and click <b>Next</b>.</li> 
<li>Enter your details:
    <ul>
    <li><b>Name</b></li>
    <li><b>Email</b>
    <p>
        <Admonition type="note">
        <p>Enter a valid email ID.</p>
        </Admonition>
    </p>
    </li>
    <li><b>Password</b></li>
    <li><b>Secret code</b></li>
    </ul>
</li>
<li>Click on <b>Sign up as mentor</b>.</li>
<li>Enter the OTP sent to your registered email ID.
<p>
    <Admonition type="tip">   
    <p>To receive a new OTP, click the <b>Didn't receive? Send another code</b> link.</p>
    </Admonition>
</p> 
</li>
<li>Do the following actions:
    <ol>
    <li>To read the privacy policy, click the <b>Privacy Policy</b> link.</li>
    <li>To read the terms of service, click the <b>Terms of Service</b> link.</li>
    </ol>
</li>
<li>Select the checkbox to agree to the terms of service and policy settings.</li>
<li>Click <b>Verify and login</b>. The Home page appears.
<p>
    <img src={PortalHomeMentor} />
</p>
</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
