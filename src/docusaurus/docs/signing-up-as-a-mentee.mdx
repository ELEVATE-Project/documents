# Signing up as a Mentee

You can create an account as a mentee if you received:

* The application's link from your organization through an email.
* Shared session or mentor profile links from your contacts.

**To sign up as a mentee, do as follows:**

<ol>
<li>Open <PartialExample mentored /> and select <b>Sign up</b>.</li>
<li>Enter your details:
    <ul>
    <li><b>Name</b></li>
    <li><b>Email</b>
    <p>
        <Admonition type="note">
        <p>Enter a valid email ID.</p>
        </Admonition>
    </p>
    </li>
    <li><b>Password</b></li>
    </ul>
</li>
<li>Click <b>Sign up</b>.</li>
<li>Enter the OTP sent to your registered email ID.</li>
<p>
    <Admonition type="tip">
    <p>To receive a new OTP, click the <b>Resend OTP</b> link.</p>
    </Admonition>
</p>
<li>Do the following actions:
    <ol>
    <li>To read the privacy policy, click the <b>Privacy Policy</b> link.</li>
    <li>To read the terms of service, click the <b>Terms of Service</b> link.</li>
    </ol>
</li>
<li>Select the checkbox to agree to the terms of service and policy settings.</li>
<li>Click <b>Verify and login</b>. The Home page appears.
<p>
    <img src={PortalHomeMentee} />
</p>
</li>
</ol>
