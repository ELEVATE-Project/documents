# Creating and Managing a Mentor Profile

On the Profile page, you can do the following actions:

<ul>
<li>Update your profile details to help mentees get to know you.</li>
<li>View the number of sessions conducted.</li>
<li>View your session ratings.</li>
</ul>

<Tabs>

## Updating your Profile

<Admonition type="note">
<p>You can use the application only after updating your profile.</p>
</Admonition>

<b>To update your profile, do as follows:</b>

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="mentored mobile application" label="Mobile App" default>
<ol>
<li>On the Profile page, tap <b>Edit profile</b>.
<br />
<br />
    <img src={EditMentorProfile} />
</li>
<li>To update your profile picture, tap <b>Add image</b> and do one of the following actions:
    <ul>
    <li>To take a picture, select <b>Take Photo</b>.</li>
    <li>To upload an image, select <b>Choose from library</b>.</li>
    <li>To remove the existing image, select <b>Remove current photo</b>.</li>
    </ul>
</li>
<li>Enter the information in the <b>Your experience in years</b>, <b>Tell us about yourself</b>, and <b>Education qualification</b> box.</li>
<li>To select your location, do as follows:
    <ul>
    <li>Tap the <b>Select your location</b> menu.</li>
    <li>Select the location and tap <b>Ok</b>.</li>
    </ul>
</li>
<li>Select an option in the <b>Your role</b>, <b>Your expertise</b>, and <b>Languages</b> box. Tap the option again to cancel the selection.
<br />
    <Admonition type="tip">
    <ul>
    <li>To add a new option, do as follows:
        <ol>
        <li>Tap <b>+ Add</b> and enter the new option.</li>
        <li>Tap <b>Ok</b>.</li>
        </ol>
    </li>
    <li>To select all the options, turn on the toggle.</li>
    </ul>
    </Admonition>
<br />
<br />
    <img src={CreateProfileOptions} />
</li>
<li>Tap <b>Submit</b>. The profile details are updated.</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
<TabItem value="mentored portal" label="Web Portal">
<ol>
<li>On the Profile page, click <b>Edit profile</b>.
<br />
<br />
    <img src={PortalEditMentorProfile} />
</li>
<li>To update your profile picture, do any one of the following actions: 
<ul>
<li>Click <b>Add image</b> and select the image.</li>
<li>To remove an existing image, click <b>Remove image</b>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Enter the information in the <b>Your experience in years</b>, <b>Tell us about yourself</b>, and <b>Education qualification</b> box.</li>
<li>To select your location, click the <b>Select your location</b> box and select the location from the menu.</li>
<li>Select an option in the <b>Your role</b>, <b>Your expertise</b>, and <b>Languages</b> box. Click the option again to cancel the selection.
<br />
    <Admonition type="tip">
    <ul>
    <li>To add a new option, do as follows:
        <ol>
        <li>Click <b>+ Add new</b> and enter the new option.</li>
        <li>Click <b>Ok</b>.</li>
        </ol>
    </li>
    <li>To select all the options, turn on the toggle.</li>
    </ul>
    </Admonition>
<br />
<br />
    <img src={PortalCreateProfileOptions} />
</li>
<li>Click <b>Submit</b>. The profile details are updated.</li>
</ol>
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
