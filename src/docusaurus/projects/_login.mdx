After signing up, you can log in to access the Home page.

<Admonition type="note">
<p>This feature is a part of the <a href="/userservice/settingup-userservice" target="_self">User Service</a>. Follow the instructions on this page if the feature is available on your application.</p>
</Admonition>

**To log in to the Home page, do as follows:**

1. Open the application and select <b>Login</b>.

2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.

    <Admonition type="note">
    <p>Enter the correct email ID and password to access the Home page. Passwords are case-sensitive.</p>
    </Admonition>

3. Click **Login**. The Home page appears.
