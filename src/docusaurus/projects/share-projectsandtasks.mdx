# Sharing Projects and Tasks

You can easily share a detailed record of your project's progress as a PDF.

To learn more about accessing and starting projects

**To share your project or tasks, do as follows:**

1. On the Project details page, do one of the following actions:

    * To share the details of a project such as the project's objective and tasks' progress, click **Share**.
    * To share the details of a task such as its progress, do as follows:
        1. Go to the **Task details** tab.
        2. Find the task.
        3. Go to **More options** and select **Share**.

2. Click **Sync and share** on the confirmation dialog.

3. To obtain a shareable link, click **Copy**.

4. Share the link with your peers or superiors.

    <Admonition type="tip">
    <p>After clicking on the shared link, they can download a PDF file containing the details of the project and tasks.</p>
    </Admonition>
