# Accessing Your Assigned Projects

You can view the details of projects that were assigned to you based on your role or location.

## Viewing Your Assigned Projects

1. Click the **Project** tile.

2. To view the project's details and tasks, do one of the following actions:

    * Go to the **Assigned to me** tab and select a project.

        <Admonition type="tip">
        <p>You can look for assigned sessions using the <b>Search</b> box.</p>
        </Admonition>

    * Click the assigned project's link.
    * Scan the assigned project's QR code using the mobile app.

        <Admonition type="info">
        <p>For more information on installing the mobile app, see Getting started.</p>
        </Admonition>

   <Tabs>
   <TabItem value="project" label="Project Details" default>
    <p>On the <b>Project details</b> tab, you can view the following details:</p>
    <ul>
    <li>Project's information such as objective, recommended for, and duration.</li>
    <li>If the Project includes certificates, the criteria for earning certificates are specified.</li>
    </ul>
    <img src={ProjectDetails} />
   </TabItem>
   <TabItem value="task" label="Task Details">
   <p>On the <b>Task details</b> tab, you can view the following details:</p>
    <ul>
    <li>The mandatory and non-mandatory tasks.</li>
    <li>Relevant learning resources.</li>
    <li>Scheduled date of completion, if specified.</li>
    </ul>
    <img src={TaskDetails} />
   </TabItem>
   </Tabs>

## Starting Your Assigned Projects

After reading the project's details and tasks, you can initiate the project.

To take up the assigned project, do the following actions:

1. Click **Start Improvement**.

2. If the project includes a certificate, verify your profile details and do as follows:
   * Click **Confirm**.
   * Click **Edit** to update your details on the Profile page.
