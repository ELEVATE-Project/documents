# FAQ on Projects

<details>

<details>
<summary>Where can I find projects that are assigned to me?</summary>

You can view your assigned projects on the **Assigned to me** tab of the Projects page.

To learn more, see <a href="viewprojects">Viewing Projects</a>.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I find project templates on the application?</summary>

From the project library, you can discover project templates on the **Library**. 

To learn more, see <a href="projectlibrary">Project Library</a>.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How can I take up projects that I have discovered through links or QR codes?</summary>

After discovering projects using links or QR codes, you can click **Start improvement** to take up the project.

To learn more, see <a href="discoverprojects">Discovering Projects</a>.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Where can I keep a track of my projects?</summary>

On the Improvement Projects Reports page, you can view a summary of your assigned, ongoing, and completed projects.

To learn more, see <a href="projectreport">Viewing Project Reports</a>.

</details>
